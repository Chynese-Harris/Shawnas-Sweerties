How can I order a cake from Shawna's Sweets?

If you wish to place an order for a specialty cake, e-mail your request to consultations@shawnassweets.com or phone 555-555-5555, prompt 1 directly.

Do you ship cakes nationwide?

Yes, we do ship our cakes nationwide.

Do I need to book a consultation?

If the cake you'd like purchase is similar to one of our cakes in our cake gallery you do not need to book a consultation. Please call us for more information at 201-448-8400 (prompt 1) during our business hours, Mon-Fri 9-5.

How can I teack my baked goods?

You will receive an email confirmation when your package leaves our facility which will include a tracking number for you to track the location of your package.

Do your products contain nuts?

Shawna's Sweets is not a nut-free environment, so we suggest that anyone with any kind of allergy refrains from consuming our products. Even if a product does not contain nuts, there is always the possibility of cross-contamination.
