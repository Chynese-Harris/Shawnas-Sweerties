Shawna's Sweets, is known for our delicious pastries, desserts & cakes. Visit us from across the nation in one of our many locations to taste our sweets or in the comfort of your own home by simply ordering online! Shawna's Sweets is a family owned bakery that was established in 2007. Shawna's Sweets, originally opened by Sarah Hawkins in 1910, was acquired by Shawna Hawkins-Lane, Sarah's daughter in 1964. Since the untimely passing of Shawna Hawkins-Lane. in 1994, matriarch Lisa Lane and her children Grace, Madelin, Mary, Lina and master baker Joseph Lane Jr. have expanded the business with the help of their spouses. Because of the Hawkins family's dedication to quality and excellence, Shawna's Sweets has received national recognition. Lisa Lane has been featured in numerous publications for her intricate sugar art designs specialty cakes and wedding cakes that taste just as good as they look.Shawna's Sweets now has 2 locations in New Jersey, 1 location in New York and 1 location in Florida!
